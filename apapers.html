Failiure And Video Game Monetisation (2024)

Discusses how video game companies use failure as a tool for monetisation, the historical causes for failure within video games, what different types of failure exist that can be exploited and how users are affected. Case studies include Genshin Impact, League Of Legends & The Sims. Scored a high second (68%).

Technologies Of Surveillance And How They Are Socially And Ethically Used (2023)

Discusses different types & methods of technological state surveillance and how data is gathered on human populations in reference to Giles Deleuze's 'Society Of Control' and Jeremy Bentham's 'Panopticon'. Case studies include XKEYSCORE, Phone Tracking in China and Denmark's Asylum System. Scored a first (78%).

The Expectations Of Futurology: Science Fiction (2022)

Discusses the concept of Futurology and compares perspectives of Solutionism to Science Fiction. Sword Art Online (anime) is used as a case study to reveal the way in which futurology is shaped by social debate and criticism. Scored a first (79%).
