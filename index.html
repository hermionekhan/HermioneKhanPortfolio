Profile

Hi! My name is Hermione Khan, I am 21 years old, my pronouns are she/her and my MBTI is ENTP. My friends percieve me as welcoming, resillient and creatively innovative (according to a recent Instagram poll) and while they are correct, I have a few more things to add: I am idealistic, driven and a perfectionist. While these words can have double edges, I prefer to use my traits and passions to my advantage!

From a young age I have travelled: From overlanding in Northern Africa to backpacking through South-East Asia, I have visited a broad range of cultures, biomes, societies, ecosystems and cities that have informed my passion for exploration. This expanded my individualism, self-sufficiency and ability to mediate and empathise with multiple perspectives.

I am happy, hopeful and hardworking. I strive to further understand what's in front of me, and even seek out what's far away. If I work for you, I will use every part of my character to achieve a result that is not one-sided, but multi-dimensional in its aim!

This Website

This website is completely hardcoded, made to showcase my work in a format of my creative ideals making it part of the portfolio!

The logo featured at the top was made in Blender and converted into a .gif format for ease of use. Many of the elements in this website were informed by my design style, which includes dynamic, clean and metallic representations. As I have the desire for perfection, this website will probably change and be altered many, many times and never conclude. Tune in for its evolution!
