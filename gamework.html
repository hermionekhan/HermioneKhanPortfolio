HCI Research Study

This experiment studied how different types of online media forms influence momentary emotion in response to influencer content. In a 1 X 3 between-participants design, a convenience sample of 36 university students (27 female, 8 male, 1 non-binary) was equally randomised into three media form conditions: long-form, short-form and audio-form.

Academic Writing
