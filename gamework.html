I specialise in narrative design, producing, marketing and environmental storytelling. Please use the email address and CV provided in the contact bar above.

Phantom Maternal

An interactive, hybrid novel which utilises the power of fictive text and 3D spaces in sequence to bring a story to life. A prototype designed for Nintendo Switch and made in Unity.

DEPTH

An immersive virtual space, built on contextual mystery and curated audio and lighting. Acts as a sample of a larger video game level.

The King's Meadow

An immersive and interactive personality quiz which features a branching narrative structure and a beautiful environment design, encompassed using a website and Unity WebGL to maximise user experience and accessibility.

HCI Research Study

This experiment studied how different types of online media forms influence momentary emotion in response to influencer content. In a 1 X 3 between-participants design, a convenience sample of 36 university students was equally randomised into three media form conditions: long-form, short-form and audio-form.

Academic Writing

A selection of academic writing that both analyses and criticises the contemporary game industry, as well as exploring technology's critical futures.
